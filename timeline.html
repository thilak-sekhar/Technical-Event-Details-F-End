2010
Inception of TechVolution

    The first TechVolution conference is held in BANGLORE.
    The event attracts tech enthusiasts, industry professionals, and innovators from across the globe.
    Keynote speakers include prominent figures in the tech industry, setting the stage for future editions of the conference.

2012
Expansion and Growth

    Due to increasing demand and popularity, TechVolution expands to a larger venue.
    The conference features a broader range of topics and sessions, covering emerging technologies and trends.









2018
Focus on Diversity and Inclusion

    TechVolution launches initiatives to promote diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.
    The conference features panels and discussions on topics such as women in tech, diversity in leadership, and accessibility in technology.









2020
Virtual Conference

    In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, TechVolution pivots to a virtual format.
    The conference successfully transitions online, attracting a record number of attendees from around the world.










2022
Hybrid Event Model

    TechVolution adopts a hybrid event model, combining in-person and virtual experiences.
    Attendees have the option to participate in-person or remotely, providing flexibility and accessibility for all.














2023
Sustainability Initiatives

    TechVolution launches sustainability initiatives to reduce its environmental footprint.
    The conference implements measures such as waste reduction, carbon offsetting, and promoting eco-friendly practices among attendees and exhibitors.








2024
TechVolution 2.0

    TechVolution celebrates its 15th anniversary with the launch of TechVolution 2.0.
    The conference features an expanded lineup of speakers, interactive experiences, and networking opportunities.
    TechVolution 2.0 marks a new era of innovation, collaboration, and inspiration in the tech industry.
